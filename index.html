About Us

The legacy of our firm began with Mr. Himmatlal Mulani, who embarked on his legal career in 1978. A dedicated lawyer and a passionate public servant, Mr. Mulani also held a deep interest in politics. His commitment to public service led him to participate in various elections, and in 1990, he was elected as a Member of the 8th Gujarat Legislative Assembly. He had the distinguished honor of serving as the Speaker of the Assembly from 11th February 1991 to 21st March 1995. Following his tenure, he returned to his legal practice with the same vigor and dedication.


Carrying forward this esteemed legacy, Dr. Mahesh H. Mulani joined the legal profession in 1998, establishing himself in the field of Civil, Criminal, Revenue, Panchayat and Cooperative Law and actively practicing before both trial and appellate courts.


The tradition continues with the next generation—Mr. Malav Mulani and Mrs. Mansi Malav Mulani—who have pursued their legal careers with the same passion and commitment. Both are currently practicing primarily before the Hon'ble High Court of Gujarat, upholding the family's longstanding values of integrity, justice, and service.


Together, we represent decades of legal expertise, a deep-rooted commitment to our clients, and an unwavering dedication to the pursuit of justice.
